Gravity and Space

I think I'm drawn to the exploration of space and gravity innately. Being aware of how i move through the world is important for me to be reminded to think about the physics of time and space. Thinking about the immensity of the universe and all of its unknowns keeps me wondering if there must be alternate realities and/or universes. Inter stellar communication must be possible, right? There must be some sort of frequency of communication that humans will probably never be able to detect because we don't have the brain power to imagine that something so stunning is possible. Propreoception is a skill that must be practiced at all times. It's good for your mind, and it helps avoid physical injuries. Plus, practicing balance and posture techniques help to make a positive difference in other aspects of life. Being Propreoception aware releives anxiety, too.
